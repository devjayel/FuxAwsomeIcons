Animations

Enhance your icons with animations by adding the relevant classes:

  • Beat: Makes the icon pulse at a steady rate.

Styles

FuxAwsomeIcons are available in several styles:

  • Solid: fa-solid
  • Regular: fa-regular

To use a different style, replace fa-solid with the desired style class.
